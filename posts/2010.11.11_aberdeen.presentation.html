Presentation @ Aberdeen University 11.11.2010

On Friday 5th November 2010 I gave a presentation on my research interests to members of the Department of Computing Science @ the University of Aberdeen. The talk was quite short and mainly covered my research interests and plans. It started with a brief overview of the core areas that I am interested in from both a theoretic and an applied perspective before I discussed a range of projects and activities that I have involved in over the last few years.
