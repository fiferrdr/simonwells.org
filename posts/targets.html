Journals, Conferences, & Workshop Series 02.04.2013

Partly an aspirational list, partly an aide memoire for when I am trying to decide where to send a piece of work... This is a useful list of journals, conferences, and workshops series that are important in my area of Artificial Intelligence research. Because of overlap with pedagogic aspects of argumentation and dialogue systems, this list also includes venues related to e-Learning and Digital Media.
